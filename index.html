Privacy Policy — EngQuiz

Last updated: 14 September 2025

EngQuiz (“the App”) is an offline English learning application. This Privacy Policy explains how user data is handled. Protecting your privacy is important to us.

Summary

  • The App does not collect, share, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data.
  • The App works completely offline and does not require an internet connection.
  • All progress and scores are stored locally on the user’s device.

Data Collection

EngQuiz does not collect any personal information. The only data stored is limited to:

  • Learning progress and scores — quiz results and progress indicators.
  • App settings — preferences such as theme or difficulty level.

This data is stored locally on the device only. It is not transmitted to us or to third parties.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. No information leaves your device.

Children’s Privacy

The App is safe for children. No personal information is collected. Parents can clear app data or uninstall the App at any time.

Your Rights

Because no personal data is collected or stored remotely, you can manage your data directly on your device by clearing the app’s storage or uninstalling the App.

Security

All information remains on your device. We recommend enabling device-level security features such as passcodes or biometrics.

Changes

If the App’s functionality changes in the future and requires data collection or internet use, this Privacy Policy will be updated and clearly communicated within the App and on this page.
